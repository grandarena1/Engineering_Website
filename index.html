About Us

CC Engineering is based in Grimsby, Lincolnshire and has been established
for over 90 years and employ some of the best trained and professionally
qualified engineers, many of whom have won national industry awards.

Working to the most stringent Health and Safety standards demanded by the food industry,
national hotel chains, contractors and councils, the company is totally committed to a first
class service supplying top quality equipment and providing “Best Value” in all instances for
our clients.
